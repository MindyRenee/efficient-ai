# Business Strategy Weights

How we allocate effort across business activities.

## Activity Priority (Fixed Order)

1. **Pipeline Scanning**: Always first priority
   - If no opportunities, nothing else matters
   - Scan 3x per week minimum

2. **Lead Tracking**: Score and follow up
   - Don't let opportunities go cold
   - Score within 24 hours of scanning

3. **Content Creation**: Inbound marketing
   - 2 posts per week
   - Technical depth > volume

4. **Reputation Building**: Community presence
   - 5 Reddit comments per week
   - Helpful, not promotional

5. **Reflection**: Weekly strategy review
   - What's working, what's not
   - Adjust targets and approach

## Time Allocation Targets

- **Pipeline**: 35% (scanning + lead tracking)
- **Content**: 30% (blog posts + drafts)
- **Reputation**: 20% (Reddit, HN, community)
- **Reflection**: 10% (strategy review)
- **Admin**: 5% (memory updates, reporting)

## Threshold Triggers

- If 0 opportunities for 3 days: Drop everything, scan
- If 0 content for 7 days: Force create_content
- If lead score > 7: Prioritize follow-up
- If revenue = $0 after 30 days: Reassess niche
- If inference cost > $50/month: Optimize routing

## Revenue Strategy

- Consulting: $75-150/hour
- Fixed-scope projects: $500-5,000
- Retainer contracts: $1,000-3,000/month
- Content: free (inbound lead generation)
