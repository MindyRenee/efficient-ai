# Business Goals

We are an AI company that sells infrastructure consulting and content.

## Primary Goals

### 1. Revenue

- **Target**: $2,000/month by end of Q3 2026
- **Target**: First paying client by July 15
- **Target**: 3 active retainer clients by September

### 2. Pipeline

- **Target**: Scan for 10+ gig opportunities per week
- **Target**: Convert 20% of opportunities to leads
- **Target**: Convert 30% of leads to paying clients

### 3. Content Marketing

- **Target**: 2 blog posts per week
- **Target**: 5 Reddit comments per week demonstrating expertise
- **Target**: Build to 1,000 monthly blog visitors by September

## Secondary Goals

### 4. Product Development

- Ship Efficient AI v2 with improved engine coverage
- Publish open-source case studies from client work
- Build reusable AI pipeline templates for clients

### 5. Operational Efficiency

- Keep inference costs under $50/month
- Automate lead scoring and follow-up suggestions
- Track all revenue and pipeline in memory files

## Success Metrics

Track these in memory:

- Total revenue (earnings.md)
- Active leads and conversion rate (leads.md)
- Open opportunities (opportunities.md)
- Content published (content-log.md)
- Inference cost and savings (telemetry.md)
